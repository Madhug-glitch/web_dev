Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through India

Founded in 2010, Spice Garden brings the authentic flavors of India to your table. Our journey began with a simple desire to share the rich culinary traditions of India with our local community.

Our head chef, with over 20 years of experience, carefully curates each dish to ensure an authentic experience. We source the finest spices directly from different regions of India to create the perfect blend of flavors that will transport you straight to the streets of Mumbai, the royal kitchens of Rajasthan, or the coastal shores of Kerala.

At Spice Garden, we believe that food is not just about taste; it's about culture, tradition, and bringing people together. We invite you to join us on this flavorful journey.

Our Specialties

North Indian Delicacies

Rich, creamy curries, tandoori specialties, and bread varieties that capture the essence of North Indian royal kitchens.

South Indian Flavors

Light, aromatic dishes with coconut and curry leaves, featuring dosas, idlis, and tangy sambar preparations.

Street Food Classics

Authentic chaats, snacks, and finger foods that bring the vibrant street food culture of India to your plate.
